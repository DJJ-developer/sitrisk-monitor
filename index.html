Why Monitor Sitting Health?

Understanding the risks of prolonged sitting

❤️ Cardiovascular Disease

Prolonged sitting increases risk of heart disease by up to 147%. Poor circulation and reduced heart activity are major factors.

🩺 Type 2 Diabetes

Sitting for 8+ hours daily increases diabetes risk by 90%. Reduced insulin sensitivity is a key concern.

🦴 Back Pain & Posture

80% of office workers experience back pain. Poor posture leads to chronic spine issues and muscle imbalances.

⚖️ Weight Gain & Obesity

Sedentary lifestyle reduces calorie burn by 90%. Metabolic slowdown leads to gradual weight gain.
